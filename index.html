Our Mission

At Izandla Ziyagezana Charities, we are dedicated to alleviating poverty through direct action programs that provide food, clothing, and essential services to those in need. We believe everyone deserves access to basic necessities and opportunities to improve their lives.

Daily Meal Programs

Operating 12 soup kitchens across the city serving nutritious meals.

Clothing Distribution

Providing warm clothing and blankets to homeless individuals.

Community Support

Connecting people with social services, job training, and housing assistance.
